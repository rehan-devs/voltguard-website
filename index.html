Since 1997

We're the Electricians Your GC Actually Trusts

VoltGuard Electrical started when Dale Meyers left a commercial EC to go out on his own with two guys and a service van. Twenty-seven years later, we run six crews across three states — but we still wire every panel like Dale's standing behind us watching.

We're an electrical subcontractor. That means we work for general contractors, not homeowners directly. We rough in new construction, wire remodels, pull feeders for commercial buildouts, and handle service upgrades that other subs won't touch. Our guys carry their own insurance, show up when the schedule says, and leave the job site cleaner than they found it.

We don't do sales pitches. We do walkthroughs, honest bids, and clean work. If we can't hit your number, we'll tell you on day one — not change order you on day forty.

— Dale Meyers, Founder & Master Electrician

How We Work

No Surprises on the Invoice or the Inspection.

01

Plan Review & Bid

Send us the prints. We'll review the electrical scope, check for conflicts with other trades, and get you a line-item bid — usually within 48 hours.

02

Pre-Wire Coordination

We sync with your framing schedule and other subs. We show up when the walls are open and the plumber's out of the way — not before, not after.

03

Rough-In

Boxes set, wire pulled, circuits labeled, grounds landed. Our foremen photograph every wall before drywall goes up. No guessing later.

04

Trim & Finish

Devices, fixtures, covers, panels landed and labeled. We tape off finished floors and wear booties on hardwood. Your GC shouldn't have to ask.

05

Inspection & Punch

We schedule the inspection, we're on-site for it, and we handle the punch list the same day. Our callback rate is under 2%.
